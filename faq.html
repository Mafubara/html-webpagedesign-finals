Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. For urgent technical support, please visit our forum where our community can often provide faster answers.

Do you accept guest posts or guide contributions?

Yes! We welcome contributions from experienced Xiaomi users. Please email your proposal to submissions@ximiguides.com with samples of your previous work.

Can I request a specific guide?

Absolutely. We prioritize guide requests based on community interest. Submit your request through our contact form or vote on existing requests in our forum.

Are you affiliated with Xiaomi?

No, Ximi Guides is an independent resource created by Xiaomi enthusiasts. We're not officially affiliated with Xiaomi Corporation.
